Our Mission

The primary goal of The Young Scientist is to foster scientific literacy amongst high school students by honing their critical thinking and analytical skills through the process of reading, writing, and synthesizing research papers concerning all subfields within science.

The Young Scientist serves as a platform for students to write review articles summarizing and discussing scholarly scientific research, publish their own original research papers with the opportunity to have it peer-edited, and access tools to assist them in their research process.
